## 1. Overview: TikTok Shop & Affiliate Ecosystem

### Understanding the Difference

| Feature | TikTok Shop | TikTok Affiliate |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Business Model | You source, list, and sell your own products | You promote other sellers' products and earn commission |
| Inventory | You handle inventory, shipping, and customer service | No inventory management - you only create content |
| Requirements | Business registration, legal documentation, US phone number | 5,000+ followers (for official program), 18+ years old |
| Setup Complexity | More complex (business verification, product listings) | Simpler (focus on content creation and promotion) |
| Profit Structure | Higher potential profits but more operational costs | Lower effort with commission-based earnings (5-20%) |
| Best For | Those with products to sell or dropshipping plans | Content creators wanting to monetize their audience |

## 2. TikTok Shop & Affiliate Account Setup

### Setting Up TikTok Shop (Seller Account)

1.   Create Your TikTok Shop Seller Account

    *   Go to [TikTok Seller Center](https://seller-us-accounts.tiktok.com/account/register)
    *   Click "Sign Up" and enter your phone number or email address
    *   Enter verification code and create a strong password
    *   Click "Register Now" to access business information form

2.   Complete Business Verification

    *   Select business type (Individual or Business)
    *   Enter business information (name, address, EIN/SSN last 4 digits)
    *   Upload verification documents (ID, business license if applicable)
    *   Complete identity verification with photo ID and selfie
    *   Agree to TikTok's Terms & Conditions and submit application

3.   Connect Bank Account

    *   Navigate to "Finance" section in Seller Center
    *   Click "Add Bank Account" and enter account details
    *   Verify ownership via micro-deposits or instant verification

4.   Configure Shop Profile

    *   Navigate to "Shop Profile" in Seller Center
    *   Upload shop logo (400x400px recommended)
    *   Write compelling shop description (200-300 characters)
    *   Add shop policies (shipping, returns, etc.)
    *   Link your TikTok account to enable content promotion

### Setting Up TikTok Affiliate (Multiple Approaches)

Option A: Official TikTok Shop Affiliate Program (5,000+ followers)

*   Ensure you meet eligibility: 5,000+ followers, 18+ years old, account in good standing
*   Open TikTok app → Profile → hamburger menu (≡) → "Creator tools" → "TikTok Shop"
*   Select "Affiliate Program" and complete application
*   Wait for approval (typically 1-7 days)
*   Once approved, access Affiliate Marketplace to select products

Option B: Alternative Route for <5,000 Followers

*   Complete TikTok Shop seller setup first
*   Navigate to "Affiliate" in Seller Center
*   Select products to make available for affiliates
*   Set commission rates (5-20% recommended)
*   Create QR codes for products
*   Create a second TikTok account for content creation
*   Build content on second account focused on niche
*   Network with other sellers for cross-promotion

Option C: Direct Brand Partnerships (Any Follower Count)

*   Create a content creator account
*   Develop a content niche (15-30 high-quality videos)
*   Research brands with TikTok Shops in your niche
*   Create a media kit highlighting your content quality
*   Reach out to brands directly via TikTok or email
*   Negotiate commission agreements (10-30% typical)
*   Request custom affiliate links or promo codes

#### About Buyer Intent Score

Our AI analyzes comments for signals that indicate purchase intent based on:

*   Explicit purchase language ("I need this", "would buy")
*   Comment engagement (likes, replies)
*   Emotional intensity indicators (emojis, exclamation marks)
*   Social proof phrases ("my friends would love this")

#### POD Design Specifications

*    T-shirt designs: 4500 x 5400 px at 300 DPI
*    Transparent PNG background for versatility
*    Color mode: RGB for vibrant digital printing
*    Front designs typically 12" x 16" print area

*    Gaming: Bold colors, cultural references
*    Pet lovers: Emotional connection, cute imagery
*    Fitness: Motivational quotes, clean design
*    Programming: Inside jokes, monospace fonts

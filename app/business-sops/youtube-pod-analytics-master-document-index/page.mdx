# YouTube POD Analytics

## Master Document Index & Download Center

April 30, 2025

This document provides a comprehensive index of all project specifications and documentation for the YouTube POD Analytics platform. Use this as your central reference for Vercel AI implementation.

## Vercel AI Project Description Prompt

Copy and paste the following prompt when submitting your project to Vercel AI:

Build a YouTube POD Analytics platform with these specific requirements:

1. Core YouTube Integration:
   - Sync 1,500+ YouTube channels and 200+ playlists with quota management
   - Extract and analyze comments for buyer intent signals 
   - Create dashboard with channel metrics and engagement statistics

2. Print-on-Demand Opportunity Detection:
   - Use AI to identify comments indicating purchase intent (e.g., "I need this on a shirt")
   - Score and rank opportunities by engagement metrics
   - Tag and categorize potential product ideas

3. Multi-Model AI Design Generation:
   - Allow simultaneous selection of multiple AI models (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion)
   - Generate and compare design variations side-by-side
   - Provide prompt templates and optimization for POD products

4. Marketplace Intelligence:
   - Integrate with Amazon, Shopify, eBay APIs for market research
   - Analyze trend data, bestseller ranks, and pricing information
   - Provide niche opportunity identification across platforms

5. Cross-Platform Data Collection:
   - Gather data from TikTok, Instagram, and other social platforms
   - Unified search across all platforms
   - Comparative performance metrics

6. Brand Management System:
   - Store brand profiles with design preferences
   - Save and organize design prompts
   - Tag products with brand associations

7. Technical Requirements:
   - Next.js application with app router
   - Supabase for database and authentication
   - Responsive dashboard interface
   - PDF/CSV/Google Sheets export options

This platform helps identify print-on-demand product opportunities from YouTube comments and validates them with marketplace intelligence before generating optimal designs.

Once deployed, add your API keys in the Vercel project settings.

## 1. Project Overview

The YouTube POD Analytics platform is a comprehensive tool for identifying print-on-demand (POD) product opportunities from YouTube comments and validating them against marketplace data. It handles large volumes of data (1,500+ subscribed channels, 200+ playlists) and provides AI-powered analysis.

### 1.1 Core Features

#### YouTube Data Synchronization

Sync channel subscriptions, playlists, videos, and comments with optimized YouTube API quota management.

#### Comment Analysis

Advanced analysis of comments to detect buyer intent and identify POD product opportunities.

#### Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive dashboard for visualizing channel performance, engagement metrics, and POD opportunities.

#### Marketplace Intelligence

Integration with Amazon, Shopify, eBay, and Google Merchant for market validation of product ideas.

#### Design Generation

AI-powered design generation for POD products based on identified opportunities.

#### AI Agents

Multiple AI agents for buyer intent detection, product validation, and market intelligence.

#### Infinite Canvas Design

Spatial organization of designs with Canva integration and two-way synchronization.

#### Export Capabilities

Export data to CSV, PDF, and Google Sheets with two-way sync support.

### 1.2 Target Users

The platform is designed for print-on-demand business owners who want to identify product opportunities from YouTube content and validate them against marketplace data.

### 1.3 Business Requirements

*   Handle large volume of YouTube data (1,500+ channels, 200+ playlists)
*   Efficiently manage YouTube API quota limitations
*   Identify high-probability POD product opportunities
*   Validate opportunities against marketplace data
*   Generate product designs and mockups
*   Provide comprehensive analytics and reporting
*   Support brand management and organization
*   Enable cross-platform social media analysis

async function syncChannels() {
  // Get all subscribed channels with pagination
  const channels = await youtubeClient.fetchAllSubscribedChannels();
  
  // Store in database
  await db.channels.bulkUpsert(channels);
  
  // Return success status

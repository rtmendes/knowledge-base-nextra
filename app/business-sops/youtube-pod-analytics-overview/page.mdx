## Alternative Buyer Intent Detection

### Beyond Explicit Comments

While explicit comments like "I need that on a shirt" provide clear buyer intent, the platform goes beyond these obvious signals to identify more subtle indicators of purchase potential across multiple signals and platforms.

### Engagement Pattern Analysis

#### View Duration Correlation

Identifies videos where users spend significantly more time watching content related to specific products or concepts, indicating higher interest levels.

#### Rewatch Behavior

Detects when viewers return to specific timestamps or rewatch videos multiple times, suggesting strong interest in particular elements or concepts.

#### Comment-to-View Ratio

Analyzes videos with unusually high comment engagement relative to view count, identifying content that provokes stronger viewer response and potential buying interest.

#### Cross-Video Engagement

Tracks when viewers engage with multiple related videos in a sequence, indicating sustained interest in a topic or concept that may translate to purchase intent.

**Content:** Segment featuring retro controller designs

### Emotional Response Indicators

#### Sentiment Analysis

Evaluates the emotional tone in comments to identify strong positive reactions to products or concepts, even when not explicitly stating purchase intent.

#### Enthusiasm Markers

Detects linguistic patterns that indicate high enthusiasm, such as exclamation points, all-caps, or repeated expressions of admiration.

#### Question Patterns

Identifies when viewers ask specific questions about products, such as "Where did you get that?" or "Is that available somewhere?", indicating purchase interest.

#### Community Reinforcement

Analyzes when multiple viewers respond positively to the same element or when comment threads develop around specific items, indicating broader appeal.

#### Emotional Response Example

### Implicit Signal Framework

#### Timestamp Concentration

Detects when comments cluster around specific video timestamps, indicating heightened interest in particular content segments that may contain product opportunities.

#### Sharing Behavior

Analyzes when users share content with specific timestamps or references, which often indicates products or concepts they find particularly compelling.

#### External Link Patterns

Identifies when viewers search for or share links to similar products in comments, suggesting they're actively looking to purchase something similar.

#### Creator Response Engagement

Tracks unusually high engagement with creator responses about products or designs, indicating heightened viewer interest in those items.

#### Weighted Signal Importance

Combines multiple signals with appropriate weighting to create a comprehensive view of buyer intent, even when no single signal is definitive on its own.

#### Context-Specific Valuation

Adjusts the importance of different signals based on the specific context, such as video type, audience, or product category, for more accurate intent detection.

#### Temporal Pattern Recognition

Identifies how signals evolve over time, detecting growing interest or emerging trends that indicate increasing purchase potential.

Correlates signals across multiple platforms to validate interest and intent, providing a more robust assessment of purchase potential.

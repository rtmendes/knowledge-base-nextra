# LinkedIn 30-Post Content Calendar — PRD

## Overview & Business Goal

Build a 30-post LinkedIn content calendar that simultaneously builds Rashida's personal brand and generates inbound leads for all four businesses: Second Spring, Family Gift Studio (FGS), ShineOn Jewelry, and InsightProfit/Polsia.

**Primary Goal:** LinkedIn is the highest-ROI organic channel for a multi-business operator. One LinkedIn post can generate consulting inquiries, partnership proposals, product sales, and email subscribers — all from the same piece of content.

**Revenue Mechanism:** Indirect. LinkedIn builds:
- **Authority** → consulting/speaking opportunities
- **Trust** → product sales from warm audience
- **Network** → partnership deals, JV opportunities
- **Traffic** → inbound to funnels, email list growth

**Target:** Consistent posting driving 500+ new LinkedIn followers in 30 days and 50+ email list sign-ups from LinkedIn per month.

## Content Strategy Framework

### The 4 Content Pillars (7–8 posts each)

**Pillar 1: Founder Journey & Behind-the-Scenes**
*"Show the work"* — Building multiple revenue streams in public
- Post types: milestone shares, failure/lesson posts, day-in-the-life
- Hook angle: "I'm building 4 businesses at once. Here's what Week 2 looked like."

**Pillar 2: Business Education & Insights**
*"Teach what you know"* — AI automation, POD, digital products, aviation
- Post types: how-to frameworks, numbered lists, tools breakdowns
- Hook angle: "5 things I learned launching a POD store with AI that nobody tells you"

**Pillar 3: Niche Value (Rotating by Business)**
*"Speak to your specific audiences"*
- Second Spring: perimenopause / women's health content
- Family Gift Studio: personalized gift industry insights
- ShineOn/POD: ecommerce, print-on-demand tips
- InsightProfit: AI tools, automation, business systems

**Pillar 4: Personal Brand & Story**
*"Let them know who you are"* — aviation career, life philosophy, values
- Post types: personal story, values-driven, social proof
- Hook angle: "I'm transitioning from [X] to aviation. Here's why I'm not waiting for permission."

## LinkedIn Profile Optimization (Pre-Launch)

Before posting begins, optimize the profile for conversion:

1. **Headline:** Not job title — value proposition. Example: "Building AI-powered businesses in women's health, ecommerce & aviation | Founder of Second Spring + 3 more ventures"
2. **Banner:** Custom Canva banner showing all 4 brands
3. **About Section:** 3-paragraph story: who you are → what you build → how to work with you. Include CTA to lead magnet.
4. **Featured Section:** Link to lead magnet opt-in, top product, or email list signup
5. **Experience:** Highlight founder roles, not just employment history
6. **LinkedIn Creator Mode:** Turn on for access to LinkedIn newsletter + broader organic reach

## AI Execution Notes

### PopeBot Can Execute Autonomously
- Write all 30 LinkedIn posts in full (hook + body + CTA for each)
- Optimize each post for LinkedIn algorithm (line breaks, no external links in body, CTA in comment)
- Write LinkedIn profile optimization copy (headline, about, featured section)
- Generate hashtag sets for each post (5–7 relevant tags)
- Create a posting schedule document with dates and post content
- Write LinkedIn newsletter edition 1 (launch edition)
- Draft DM templates for follow-up with engaged commenters

### Requires Human Action
- Approve all posts before scheduling
- Take/update LinkedIn profile photo if needed
- Set up Creator Mode on LinkedIn
- Upload custom banner to LinkedIn profile
- Schedule posts (Buffer/Taplio requires account access)
- Respond to comments and DMs in real-time
- Record any video posts that require on-camera presence
- Make decisions about which inbound opportunities to pursue

### LinkedIn Algorithm Notes (2026)
- Posts without external links in the body get 3–5x more reach
- Put any links in the first comment
- Post between 7–9am or 5–7pm target timezone
- Respond to every comment in the first 60 minutes to boost distribution
- Polls get 5x average impressions — use sparingly for engagement spikes
- Document/carousel posts get highest saves and shares

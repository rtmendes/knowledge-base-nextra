# Second Spring Digital Offers — PRD

## Overview & Business Goal

Second Spring is a digital health brand targeting women aged 38–55 navigating perimenopause and menopause. The business delivers high-value educational content and transformation programs that help this underserved demographic understand their bodies, manage symptoms, and thrive through the transition.

**Primary Goal:** Reach $500/day in revenue through a systematic digital product stack — low-ticket entry offers ($7–$47), mid-ticket courses ($97–$297), and high-ticket programs ($497–$997).

**Why This Works Now:** Perimenopause content is massively underserved on search and social. Women 40–55 are high-intent buyers. GHL (GoHighLevel) enables zero-platform-fee sales with full funnel control.

## Implementation Plan

### Phase 1 — Launch-Ready (This Week, Days 1–7)

**Goal:** Get first sale by Day 7.

1. **Define Core Product Stack** (PopeBot can draft)
   - Lead magnet: "The Perimenopause Symptom Decoder — 7 Signs You're in the Transition (and What to Do)"
   - Tripwire: "$27 Hormone Balance Starter Kit" — 3-module mini-course
   - Core offer: "$197 Second Spring Signature Course" — 6-week self-paced program

2. **Build GHL Funnel — Lead Magnet Flow**
   - Opt-in page with lead magnet offer
   - Thank-you page with tripwire upsell ($27)
   - Lead magnet delivery via email automation
   - 5-email welcome sequence (see Email Sequences PRD)

3. **Create Core Content Assets**
   - Lead magnet PDF (8–12 pages, Canva template)
   - Sales page copy for tripwire (PopeBot drafts from SOPs)
   - 3 social media posts for organic seeding

4. **GHL Checkout Setup**
   - Product: $27 Hormone Balance Starter Kit
   - Stripe/payment connected
   - Order confirmation + delivery email

5. **Publish Minimum Viable Content**
   - 1 YouTube video or TikTok (talking head — symptom decoder hook)
   - Pin to Instagram/Facebook

**Success Condition for Phase 1:** First paid sale through GHL checkout.

---

### Phase 2 — Growth (Next 2 Weeks, Days 8–21)

**Goal:** 5–10 sales/day, $200–$300/day revenue.

1. **Build Out Course Content**
   - Record/write 6-module Second Spring Signature Course
   - Upload to GHL membership area
   - Create course workbooks (Canva)

2. **Email Sequences**
   - Post-purchase upsell sequence (3 emails: tripwire → signature course)
   - Weekly newsletter to list (value content + soft sell)
   - Abandoned cart recovery (3 emails, GHL automation)

3. **Content Calendar Execution**
   - 3 posts/week on Instagram (Reels format)
   - 2 YouTube shorts/week (symptom tips, hormone hacks)
   - 1 long-form YouTube/week (SEO: "perimenopause symptoms", "what is perimenopause")
   - 1 TikTok/day (repurposed from Instagram Reels)

4. **Paid Traffic Test (Small Budget)**
   - $10–$20/day Meta ads → lead magnet
   - A/B test 2 ad creatives (before/after framing vs. symptom relief)
   - Track CPL (target: under $3)

5. **Add Mid-Ticket Offer**
   - Launch $97 "30-Day Hormone Reset Challenge"
   - Email blast to existing list

---

### Phase 3 — Scale (Month 2+)

**Goal:** $500/day consistently. Systemize and automate.

1. **Paid Traffic Scale**
   - Scale winning Meta ad set to $50–$100/day
   - Build YouTube ads targeting perimenopause keywords
   - Affiliate/JV partnerships with women's health coaches

2. **High-Ticket Backend**
   - Launch $497/month group coaching program
   - Application funnel from email list
   - Live cohort every 6–8 weeks

3. **SEO Content Engine**
   - 3 long-form blog posts/week targeting perimenopause search terms
   - Keyword clusters: perimenopause symptoms, hormone balance, menopause weight gain
   - Internal linking to product pages

4. **Automation & Systems**
   - All email sequences fully automated in GHL
   - PopeBot generates weekly newsletter drafts from Genspark SOPs
   - Social media scheduling via GHL or Buffer

5. **Product Expansion**
   - Add: "Perimenopause Meal Plan" digital download ($27)
   - Add: "Sleep & Hormones Masterclass" ($47)
   - Add: Annual membership ($297/year)

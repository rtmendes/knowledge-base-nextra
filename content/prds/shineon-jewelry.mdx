# ShineOn Jewelry (5 Products) — PRD

## Overview & Business Goal

Launch 5 high-converting print-on-demand jewelry products on the ShineOn platform. ShineOn specializes in personalized jewelry (necklaces, bracelets, rings, keychains) with strong gifting appeal — Mother's Day, Christmas, birthdays, and memorial occasions are the highest-converting niches.

**Primary Goal:** $300/day in net POD margin from 5 products, scaling to 15+ products in Phase 2.

**Why ShineOn:** Industry-leading margins (30–50% on jewelry vs. 10–15% on apparel POD), built-in fulfillment, and a gifting audience that converts at premium price points ($30–$80 per item).

---

## Revenue Model

| Component | Details |
|---|---|
| Revenue per sale | $30–$80 retail price |
| ShineOn base cost | $8–$20 depending on product |
| Net margin per sale | $15–$45 (avg ~$25) |
| Sales needed for $300/day | 12–20 sales/day across 5 products |
| Platform | ShineOn (primary) + Etsy or Shopify (optional) |

**Order Flow:**
1. Customer sees ad or organic post
2. Clicks to product landing page (GHL or ShineOn native)
3. Personalizes item (name, message, date)
4. Completes checkout (ShineOn processes payment + fulfillment)
5. GHL receives order trigger → post-purchase sequence fires

## Implementation Plan

### Phase 1 — Launch-Ready (This Week, Days 1–7)

**Goal:** All 5 products live on ShineOn with mockups and GHL connected.

1. **Finalize Niche & Product Validation**
   - Confirm 5 product selections above (or substitute based on current ShineOn bestsellers)
   - Check ShineOn bestseller list for current trending products
   - Verify margin calculator: retail price - base cost - ads = profitable

2. **Create Product Designs**
   - Use ShineOn's built-in design tools OR upload custom designs
   - For each product: primary design file, 2–3 message variants
   - Messages to generate per product: Daughter, Son, Wife, Mom, Memorial
   - AI can generate copy for each message variant (PopeBot task)

3. **Build ShineOn Listings**
   - Upload design to ShineOn product builder
   - Set pricing (aim for 2.5–3x base cost)
   - Write product titles: keyword-rich (e.g., "Personalized Mom Necklace Gift — Interlocking Hearts Necklace for Mother's Day")
   - Write product descriptions: SEO + emotional hook
   - Add all personalization fields (name, date, message)
   - Enable all size/variant options

4. **Generate Product Mockups**
   - Use ShineOn's mockup generator
   - Export: lifestyle mockup (model wearing), flat lay, gift box shot
   - At least 4 mockup images per product

5. **GHL Order Flow**
   - Connect ShineOn webhook or Zapier trigger to GHL
   - Build post-purchase sequence: order confirmation → shipping notification → review request → upsell email
   - Set up abandoned cart flow (if using GHL checkout)

6. **First Ad Creative**
   - 1 video ad per product (slideshow with mockup + text overlay)
   - 1 static image ad per product
   - Copy: "Looking for the perfect gift for Mom?" + product hook

**Success Condition for Phase 1:** All 5 products live; first organic or paid sale.

---

### Phase 2 — Growth (Next 2 Weeks, Days 8–21)

**Goal:** 5–10 sales/day, $100–$200/day revenue.

1. **Launch Paid Traffic**
   - Meta ads: $10/day per winning product (start with #1 and #3 — highest emotional appeal)
   - Target: Women 30–60 interested in gifting, jewelry, family
   - Retargeting: ShineOn pixel + GHL list custom audience

2. **Pinterest Organic**
   - Post all product mockups to Pinterest (free traffic, high buyer intent)
   - 5 pins per product, keyword-optimized titles
   - Board: "Personalized Jewelry Gifts", "Mother's Day Gift Ideas"

3. **Etsy Expansion (Optional)**
   - List top 3 performing products on Etsy
   - Etsy SEO optimization for jewelry gifting keywords
   - Connect ShineOn fulfillment to Etsy orders via ShineOn's Etsy integration

4. **Scale Winning Products**
   - Identify top 2 performers from Phase 1
   - Add 3–5 message variants per winner
   - A/B test pricing ($44.99 vs $49.99 vs $54.99)

5. **Email List Building**
   - Post-purchase incentive: "Get 15% off your next order — join our VIP list"
   - GHL opt-in integrated at checkout

---

### Phase 3 — Scale (Month 2+)

**Goal:** $300/day consistently. Expand to 15+ SKUs.

1. **Product Line Expansion**
   - Add 10+ new SKUs (holiday themes, sports, pets, couples)
   - Test seasonal drops: Mother's Day bundle, Christmas collection
   - High-ticket bundle: Necklace + Bracelet gift set ($89.99)

2. **Paid Traffic Scale**
   - Scale winning ad sets to $50–$100/day
   - Launch Google Shopping ads (ShineOn provides product feeds)
   - YouTube Shorts organic content (unboxing, gifting moments)

3. **Automation**
   - PopeBot generates new product message variants weekly
   - AI image generation pipeline for new mockup variations
   - Automated review request → testimonial capture → social proof integration

4. **Influencer Micro-Campaign**
   - Seed 5–10 micro-influencers (10K–50K followers) with free products
   - Request unboxing/gifting content
   - Repurpose as ad creative

5. **TikTok Shop Integration**
   - List top products on TikTok Shop
   - Short-form unboxing videos (organic + seeded)

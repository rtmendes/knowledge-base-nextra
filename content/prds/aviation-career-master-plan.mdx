#### Study Tips for Exam Success
- **3:1 Ratio:** For every 3 hours of content reading, do 1 hour of practice questions
- **Active recall:** Close notes and write from memory; don't just re-read
- **Spaced repetition:** Use Anki or physical flashcards for regulation/definition memorization
- **Simulate exam conditions:** Take practice exams timed, no notes, quiet room
- **Sleep:** 7–8 hours the week before the exam — sleep consolidates memory more than cramming
